🔍 Repetition Checker — Rules & Colour Guide

Colour Highlights

  • Orange cell ⚠ — Needs attention:
    same protein/ingredient in 2+ meals on the same day, or the same protein form served on back-to-back days.
  • Yellow cell ℹ — Frequency notice:
    any item used 4 or more times across the whole week.

Three Checking Rules

  • 🔁 Same-day — A protein or watched ingredient appears in 2 or more meals on the same day (e.g. chicken at both Lunch and Dinner).
  • 🍖 Back-to-back — The same form of a protein appears in the same meal type on consecutive days (e.g. Pork Chop at Lunch Mon & Tue). Configurable above — you can also require an exact same-meal match or flag any consecutive-day repeat.
  • 📋 Weekly frequency — Any tracked item appears 4+ times in the week.
Protein Form Rules — Same-protein forms that are distinct enough not to feel repetitive are tracked separately and will not trigger back-to-back warnings against each other:

🥩 Pork: Ham · Bacon · Chop / Loin / Roast / Pulled Pork / Brat — each form is checked independently.
🥩 Beef: Steak / Sirloin · Patty / Ground / Meatball / Salisbury · Roast / Brisket / Stew · Other Beef — each form is checked independently.
🐟 Fish & Seafood: Each species (Salmon, Tuna, Shrimp, Tilapia, Cod) is tracked separately.
🥔 Potato (main American starch): Hash Brown · Sweet Potato · Mashed · Baked · Roasted/Red · Other — each form is checked independently, same as proteins.

Watched Ingredients (frequency & same-day only, no back-to-back rule): Mushroom · Broccoli · Corn · Carrot · Rice · Pasta · Squash · Zucchini · Asparagus · Collard · Cornbread · Stuffing

Eggs are not checked — they are a common breakfast staple and expected to repeat.

Fruits (Apple, Peach, etc.) are intentionally excluded from all checks.
